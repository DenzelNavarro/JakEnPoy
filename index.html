Let's Play Jak En Poy!

Origins of the Phrase "Jak En Poy"

Jak En Poy is a Filipino variation of the globally popular hand game known as rock-paper-scissors.

While the game's basic mechanics are the same, Jak En Poy adds a distinctive cultural twist through a rhythmic chant.

"Jack en poy, hale-hale hoy! Sinong matalo, siya'ng unggoy!"

Translation: "Jak en Poy, hale-hale hoy! Whoever loses becomes a monkey!"
